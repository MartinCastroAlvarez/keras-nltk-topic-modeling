Local Markets

Our markets are lively community spaces where shoppers and food producers connect to build relationships, community health, and strong local economies.

Regional Mentor

More than a market: we develop services, tools, and programs for the needs of our diverse population and share what we learn along the way.

National Model

We cultivate the field of public markets for public good nationally, regionally, and locally in New Orleans through collective learning, sharing, and growing.
