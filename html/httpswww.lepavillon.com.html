Welcome toLe Pavillon New Orleans

A New Orleans Hotel That Radiates History

Le Pavillon hotel is in the heart of New Orleans conveniently located in the Central Business District, a short stroll to the French Quarter, Riverfront attractions, Convention Center, Superdome and major shopping. From our standard hotel rooms to the Presidential Suite, guests enjoy wonderful amenities and services in a beautiful setting. Listed on the National Register of Historic Places, our hotel is furnished with exquisite antiques and works of art. Dine in our signature restaurant, Bijoux at Le Pavillon, or in the comfort of your guest accommodations. Spend a day relaxing by the rooftop swimming pool or take part in the many annual festivals that happen in New Orleans, such as Mardi Gras​, Jazz Fest and Christmas New Orleans Style. Our concierge will be happy to assist you with your New Orleans sightseeing plans. If you're planning a New Orleans event, our elegant, flexible meeting venues are perfect for wedding receptions, business conferences and everything in between.
