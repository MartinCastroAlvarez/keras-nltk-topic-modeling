Von Eberts beautiful Pearl District location opened in March of 2018, replacing and improving on the Fat Heads location previously in the space. Hailed as one of the "Most Anticipated New Breweries of 2018", Our beautiful location in downtown Portland features beautiful arched ceilings, plentiful natural light, and the historic charm of the Pearl District with abundant seating for just under 300 people.

Von Eberts Glendoveer location opened early in the summer of 2018. Expanding on an already rapid success under the original VE leadership, the East location continues the recognizable and innovative traditions of the Pearl location in a new and beautiful location! Our East location is currently hiring for a number of positions; for more information, visit our Jobs page!
