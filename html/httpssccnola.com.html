Welcome to Southern Costume Company

Costume Rental

Southern Costume Company is your premier headquarters for incredible, affordable costume rentals. Come in and discover the magic that we live and breathe as we bring new, and some old, characters to life.

Production

Southern Costume Company makes available to the film industry a 9,000 sq. ft. warehouse containing cages, design rooms/office space, on-site seamstresses, wardrobe rentals, wardrobe/costuming supplies, street-level freight and loading.
