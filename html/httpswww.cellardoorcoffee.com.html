    Here's a video that was taken by some coffee friends who visited our roastery back in 2010. Our innovative, low-energy air cleaning method means that our roaster exhaust gets cleaner and cooler as it travels out of our building instead of being burned up in a gas-fired oven called an afterburner. Keeps our neighbors and the environment a little happier.
