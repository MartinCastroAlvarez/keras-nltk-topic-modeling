Welcome to Redwood

RESTAURANT|BAR

Redwood is located in the heart of Montavilla serving an all scratch menu.
We have a small kitchen and are always busy pickling, braising, baking, and keeping our menu inspired. We look forward to all the exciting changes happening here on Stark Street as we continue to do what we love: cook, eat, drink, live!
