Portland Public House

Our Pub’s distinct Northwest style brings a taste of Central Oregon to the heart of Portland and is a popular destination for great beer, delicious food and a relaxed urban atmosphere.

The brew pub’s +20 taps feature Deschutes mainstays plus a selection of seasonal and experimental beers developed and brewed on site exclusively for the Portland Pub by our brewer, Jake Harper.

Led by Executive Chef Jill Ramseier, our talented culinary team creates classic pub food taken to the next level. Matching our brewers’ passion, they create dishes from scratch with the best possible local ingredients.

Now featuring our signature Black Butte Whiskey, available in three varieties, individually or as a flight.  Or try our small batch series of barrel-aged Black Butte Whiskey Manhattans.  Other cocktails and beer-tails include our H-Ale Mary, with house-made Bloody Mary mix, Crater Lake Vodka and Black Butte Porter.

The Portland Public House is now accepting reservations.  RESERVE NOW For parties larger than 10, please contact the pub directly.

Love our food but want to eat at home? We’ve partnered with CAVIAR, a delivery service that will bring fresh food from our pub to your home. Click here to check out the menu and to order your next meal.

Portland Public House Events

  • Jul25

    07/25/2019

    Marionberry Lavender Sour Ale Celebration

    FUN FACT: Did you know the Marionberry was developed at Oregon State University?  Did you also know that Oregon is home to THOUSANDS of acres of lavender fields?

    What better way to showcase two magnificent homegrown plants than to combine them for a *truly* expressive sour ale?

    We’re kicking off Oregon Brewers Festival weekend celebrating Marionberry Lavender Sour Ale, the first of our upcoming Sour Series available on draft and in cans!

    Join us at the Portland Pub on Thursday, July 25th for free samples of Marionberry Lavender Sour Ale, food specials to pair, Oregon Marionberry jam and local lavender, cocktail specials and more!

  • Jul30

    07/30/2019

    Community Pints @ the Portland Public House

    Join us Tuesdays in July as we feature our local Community Pints partner, with $1 from each pint sold going directly to a wonderful organization.

    In July, we’re partnering with Caldera.  From their website:

    Caldera launched in 1996 as an arts camp in the mountains. The idea was to bring together youth with limited opportunities, from both the city and from the country, to make art. Turns out it was a pretty good idea. Youth who said they couldn’t draw or write or keep a rhythm found out they were artists. Youth who had never been that far from a town or a city found that hiking was fun and, even more, that they could use their creativity to solve life problems or care for the forest and rivers. They envisioned lives they thought were out of their reach. Students who were at risk of dropping out of school kept with it, graduated from high school, won college scholarships, and came back to work at Caldera.

    Please help us support this amazing organization – each Tuesday of July!

  • Aug15

    08/15/2019

    Handup IPA Celebration

    The handup, the most common heckling technique used on Cyclocross courses, is used to distract cyclists and entice them with donuts, bacon, candy, or even better, BEER.

    Deschutes Brewery has both professional and amateur Cyclocross teams in Bend, OR and in Roanoke, VA.  We’ve cleared our warehouse lawn in Bend for several races and even hosted national championships!

    Handup IPA is our tribute to the sport we love.  An American IPA with big hop aromas of citrus and tropical, floral, grassy, and focused on drinkability.

    We’re celebrating all day long on Thursday, August 15th with free samples, food specials, bike swag, and a chance to win some fabulous giveaways.  Stay *tuned* for more details!
