We raise a glass to Nathaniel K. West, the forward-thinking merchant responsible for the structure in which we stand today. When Mr. West arrived to Oregon, East Portland was an independent municipality, the Willamette was crossed by boat or ferry, and a bit or two would buy a jigger of whiskey.

The Latter Day Skanks, The Wild Jumps, Dartgun and The Vignettes

Saturday July 27th

Three punk bands with three different styles. Come dance, mosh, yell and drink with us at Bit House! The Latter Day Skanks - You know them because they wear fishnets, glitter, and because they shred guitar as well as shred the thin moral fabric of religion. They endeavor to make sad boys an endangered species. The Wild Jumps - You've seen them on stage celebrating cutting ties and getting pissed about the state of the world and dancing all over the place. They might sweat all over the front row, but the front row likes it. Dartgun and the Vignettes - You love them cause they bring doo-wop punk to the stage. They swing their hips, harmonize, and dazzle. They give chaos a velvet cloak and tame it. Doors at 9pm, 21+ and an 8 dollar cover. Come ready to join group therapy via punk music.
